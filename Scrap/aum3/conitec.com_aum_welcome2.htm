About this Edition

    All the more respect is due to those many ACKNEX soloists who, without the proper budget of a wealthy publisher or the support of a experienced team backing them, at home and all by themselves go about putting together their very own, private 3D Game. We know how much work goes into even the smallest of levels. And it's always a relief if a tool is made available to you that facilitates level designing - especially if you're a beginner. One such tool is the long awaited and much demanded ACKNEX-EDITOR. In this issue we for the first time proudly present (FLOURISH!) a demo to play around with! All functions are available, but you can't save anything, of course! But the software comes at a quite reasonable price. Read more about it in our column on  ACKNEX related Tools..

    The HINTS column again offers quite a few interesting tips for professionals. Among other things there you'll find a complete MOUSE wdl and tips about how to produce clickable textures. And our old colleague Chris Zoinks has made an open forum available for for a few weeks now. So enjoy!
