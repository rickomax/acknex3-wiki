CONTEST '97


2nd Level Designer Contest

    Thank you all for your ambitious deliveries. We are busy with testing now and the winner will be known 'til next issue! Keep patient and be sure that there will be another contest for 1998!

      Here is what you will win:

     
    1st Prize  3D GameStudio professional version
    2nd and 3rd Prize A book "Game Studio 3D" published by TEWI (in German) 
     
      Here are the conditions:
      • Can be run with the Lite Version
      • Level must be completely documented
      • Include a level plan in a file format of your choice

      Topographical concept as well as execution and the quality of the transposition of actions will be evaluated, but also storyline and puzzles as well as music and sound. As before teams are also allowed to enter the contest. The jury will be made up of employees of the companies Conitec and oP group as well as AUM.

    we suggest that you use "Game Studio 3D", published be TEWI to aide you. In it you will find a shareware version of the January 1996 release as well a lot of textures, sounds, and tools. But remember that only those textures that were explicity released may be used in the contest!
