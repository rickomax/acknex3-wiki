Conitec Datensysteme GmbH is a small German company, founded in 1985.

On the right side you're looking at our HQ building nearby the village of Dieburg, southeast of Frankfurt.

 Our U.S. office is located in San Diego. We're creating and producing development tools, multimedia tools and game engines for our customers.
Within our Software Section you'll find 3D GameStudio, a commercial authoring and game creation system, powered by the ACKNEX 3-D engine. Create royalty free computer games or other 3-D applications - without programming skills! The same section contains the online ACKNEX Users Magazine & Forum, where you can read everything about 3D game design, exchange experiences with other GameStudio users, and get hints from the professionals.
The pocket multiprogramming device GALEP-III is on display in our Hardware Section. Plug it to any laptop or PC and program quick and easily EPROMs, EEPROMs, Flash-EPROMs, EPLDs, and microcontrollers! You'll find there also the RAM / EPROM simulator PEPS-III.
