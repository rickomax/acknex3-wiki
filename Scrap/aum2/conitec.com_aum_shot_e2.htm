      And here are December's Screenshots of the Month!

 

      Both screenshots this time are from "PIKTO" by Phaestos, an action-game for kids.
     
What makes these shots so special to us is the unique way of dealing with textures and atmosphere. Realism was left out in this case in favour of a fairy-tale mood. All graphics are right on the mark, the way Light-Angle and Genius are used is especially clear in the right hand shot, if you take a closer look at the arcades. Apart from that the topography is made to fit hand-in-glove, created professionally and convincingly. And it doesn't pixel the slightest bit!
